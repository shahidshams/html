– How does GDPR impact digital projects?

# GDPR is set to impact digital marketing is data processing.
Digital marketers will only be able to process the data they really
need, requiring legal justification for why the personal data
they’ve collected is being processed.
In a blog post on the topic, Steve MacDonald explains why this isn’t
as bad as it sounds. Steven recommends that marketers “need to focus on
the data you need, and stop asking for the ‘nice to haves’.”
Indeed, 42% of B2B marketers believe that a lack of quality data is the
biggest barrier to lead generation. Focusing on the data that really
matters should be beneficial for both digital marketers and individuals
